About the Hotel

Panna's Gateway
to Luxury Living

Nestled in Kunjvan, Panna — Hotel Shanvi Landmark is the region's premier hospitality destination. Perfectly situated just 1.5 km from the Hinauta Gate of Panna Tiger Reserve, our hotel offers a rare blend of modern luxury and the wild heart of Bundelkhand.

Whether you're a wildlife enthusiast headed to the safari, a pilgrim visiting Panna's sacred temples, or a family celebrating life's grandest moments — Shanvi Landmark is your home away from home.

Why Panna

Panna — The City of
Diamonds, Temples & Tigers

Stay at Shanvi Landmark — the perfect base to experience everything Panna has to offer

🐯

Panna Tiger Reserve

India's 5th Tiger Reserve in MP. Jeep safari, boat safari on Ken River, tigers, leopards, sloth bears & 200+ bird species. Just 1.5km from hotel.

📍 1.5 km from hotel
🏛️

Khajuraho Temples

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Stunning Chandela dynasty temples (950–1050 AD) — Western, Eastern & Southern groups. One of India's top attractions.

📍 41 km from hotel
💎

Diamond Mines (Majhagaon)

Asia's only active large-scale diamond mine. NMDC-operated. Panna is India's diamond capital — mined for 3,000+ years. A rare experience.

📍 ~20 km from hotel
🙏

Jugal Kishoreji Temple

Panna's most sacred temple. Bundela architecture, believed to complete the Char Dham pilgrimage. Built by Raja Hindupat Singh in 1758.

📍 In Panna city
🌊

Pandav Falls & Caves

30-metre waterfall inside Panna National Park. Ancient caves at the base. Mythological link to the Pandavas' exile. Stunning year-round.

📍 12 km from Panna
🐊

Raneh Falls & Ken Gharial Sanctuary

Breathtaking 98-ft deep canyon waterfalls on Ken River. Home to rare Gharials & crocodiles. Near Khajuraho — a must-visit.

📍 20 km from Panna
